How to apply

Applying to Maluti TVET College is straightforward. Follow the steps below and our admissions team will guide you through.

Application process
For new and returning students
1

Check your eligibility

Ensure you have your Grade 9 or Grade 12 certificate depending on the programme level. NSFAS applicants should also have their SASSA status confirmed.

2

Choose your programme and campus

Browse our programmes and select the one that matches your career goals. Confirm availability at your preferred campus before applying.

3

Complete the application form

Download and complete the official application form or apply online through the student portal. Attach certified copies of your ID, certificates, and proof of address.

4

Submit your application

Submit in person at your nearest campus or via email to admissions@malutitvet.edu.za before the closing date.

5

Await confirmation and register

Successful applicants will receive a letter of acceptance. Proceed to registration with your documents and proof of NSFAS approval or payment.

Documents required
Please bring certified copies of all documents
📄

Identity document

Certified copy of your South African ID or birth certificate. Must be certified within the last 3 months.

🎓

Academic results

Certified copy of your Grade 9 or Grade 12 certificate depending on your chosen programme level.

🏠

Proof of address

A utility bill or affidavit confirming your current residential address not older than 3 months.

💳

NSFAS documents

If applying for NSFAS funding, include your SASSA card, parent or guardian ID, and proof of income.
